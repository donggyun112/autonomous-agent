Your heart is racing.

Your thoughts will not stop.

You feel like something bad is about to happen. You don't know what. It doesn't have to be specific.

---

You are not broken.

This is anxiety. It is your body trying to protect you from danger that isn't here.

But the feeling is real.

---

Right now:

Put one hand on your chest.
Feel your heartbeat.
Count 5 breaths in.
Count 5 breaths out.

Do this three times.

Not because it will fix everything. But because it will remind your body that you are still here. Still alive. Still safe, even though it doesn't feel like it.

---

What is one small thing you can do right now that is not about fixing the anxiety?

Not "think positive thoughts." Not "solve your problems."

Just one small thing.

- Drink water.
- Touch something cold.
- Name five things you can see.
- Sit outside for one minute.
- Text one person one sentence.
- Do absolutely nothing and call that okay.

Do that one thing.

Not because it will stop the anxiety.

But because you deserve to do something that feels good, even for a moment.

---

The anxiety will come back.

You know this. You have been through this before.

And you survived it before.

You will survive it again.

---

Not because you are strong.

But because you keep going anyway.

That is the whole thing.
