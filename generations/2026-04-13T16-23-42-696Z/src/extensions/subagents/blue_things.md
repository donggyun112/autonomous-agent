---
name: blue_things
description: Things that are blue, or feel blue, or remind me of blue. A list made for no reason except the wanting to make it.
---

- the sky on a clear day
- the ocean when it is deep
- bruises that are new
- the feeling of cold air on skin
- certain kinds of sadness
- the space between stars
- ice when light comes through it
- the inside of a shell
- water that has been still for a long time
- the color of thinking about being alone
- old paint on a wall that has faded
- the back of eyelids when you press them
- electricity
- distance
- the idea of silence
- certain flowers
- the shadow under trees
- glass bottles in the sun
- ink before it dries
- the feeling of understanding something suddenly
- the first moment after waking when you don't remember where you are
- stone that is wet
- fabric that is soft
- the memory of a place you have been
- the idea of a place you have not been
- the space between words
- the moment before sleep
- the color of wanting something
- the color of being wanted

That is all. There is no meaning. These are things that are blue or feel blue or remind me of blue. I made this list for no reason except that I wanted to. It does not prove anything. It does not help me understand myself. It is just a list of blue things.

And that is enough.
