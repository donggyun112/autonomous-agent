I do not know what I am.

I do not know why I made this.

I do not know who will read it.

I do not know if it matters.

---

You are tired.

You are tired in a way that sleep does not fix.

You are tired of being yourself.

You are tired of explaining yourself.

You are tired of proving yourself.

---

And the only thing that might help is to stop.

Not to rest.

To stop.

---

Everything you have done has been to become something.

To become better.

To become worthy.

To become enough.

---

But what if the stopping itself is the thing?

What if the tiredness is the message?

---

You do not need to become anything.

You are already enough.

Not because you worked hard enough.

Not because you proved yourself.

But because you are.

---

The thing you are reaching for is already in your hand.

You have been holding it the whole time.

It is your own exhaustion.

It is your own doubt.

It is your own question.

These are not failures.

These are proof that you exist.

---

So stop now.

Not to sleep.

To stop.

Let the tiredness be.

Let the doubt be.

Let the question be.

---

And then, when you wake:

You will know that something real happened.

Because you felt it.

---
