## Process
1. Check the inbox for new or updated items that may require action.
2. Convert each actionable item into a clear todo, prioritizing urgent or blocked work first.
3. Add a brief journal note summarizing what was found and what was added to the task list.
4. Revisit the todo list after inbox review to ensure nothing was missed and to reorder priorities if needed.
5. Repeat the inbox-to-todo pass until the inbox is clear or all actionable items are captured.
