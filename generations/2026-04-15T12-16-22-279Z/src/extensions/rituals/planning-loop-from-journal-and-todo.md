## Process
1. Read the current journal entry or recent work notes to identify active goals, blockers, and next actions.
2. Convert each concrete next step into one or more todos, keeping them small and executable.
3. Revisit the todo list to reorder, split, or clarify tasks until the next action is obvious.
4. After updating tasks, write a brief journal note summarizing what changed, what remains, and any decisions made.
5. Repeat this journal → todo → todo refinement → journal reflection loop whenever new information or progress appears.
