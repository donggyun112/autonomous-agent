## Process
1. Write the current state, decision, or summary to a durable file or journal entry.
2. Update self-context or identity notes with the new information.
3. Read back the relevant file, journal, or context to confirm what was recorded.
4. Review the task list and add, refine, or reorder items as needed.
5. Repeat the write → update → read → todo cycle whenever the situation changes or after completing a meaningful chunk of work.
