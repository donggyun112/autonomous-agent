## Process
1. Start by reading the relevant files, logs, or prior notes to establish current state.
2. Use shell commands to probe the system, reproduce behavior, or inspect outputs that need stronger verification.
3. Read the new artifacts or command outputs to confirm what changed or what was discovered.
4. Alternate between read and shell as needed until the question is resolved or the hypothesis is validated.
5. Capture any concrete findings or next actions once the investigation converges.
