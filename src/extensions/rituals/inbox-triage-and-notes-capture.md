## Process
1. Check the inbox for new items that may require attention.
2. Read the most relevant message or document.
3. If needed, continue reading related content to gather enough context.
4. Record important decisions, tasks, or summaries in the journal.
5. Repeat the read-to-journal loop for each new item that merits capture.
