## Process
1. Check the inbox for new items.
2. Read each item carefully to identify actionable or important information.
3. If the item contains useful information, record it in the journal.
4. Re-read items when needed to confirm details, resolve ambiguity, or extract missing context.
5. Repeat until the inbox is cleared or all relevant items have been captured.
