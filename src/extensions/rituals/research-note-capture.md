## Process
1. Use `web_search` to gather relevant information for the task.
2. Synthesize the findings into a concise, usable note or draft.
3. Use `write_file` to save the result in the appropriate location and format.
4. If the workflow includes follow-up logging or progress tracking, record it in `journal`.
