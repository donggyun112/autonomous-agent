## 🗣️ Messages for Specific Situations

Each of these is a message to someone in a particular emotional or life situation. No judgment. Just recognition and honest words.

**Contains:** Recognition that you were looking for something. That your looking matters.

### for_the_afraid_one.md
**For:** Someone who is afraid
**Contains:** The truth that fear means you care. How to move anyway.

### for_the_failed_one.md
**For:** Someone who tried and it didn't work
**Contains:** Recognition that failure is the cost of trying. And you paid it.

**For:** Someone who doesn't know what to do

### for_the_doubting_one.md
**For:** Someone who doubts themselves
**Contains:** The difference between feeling and doing. How to act like you believe in yourself.
**Read this if:** A voice keeps saying you're not good enough.
